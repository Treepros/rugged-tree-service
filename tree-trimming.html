FAQs AND ANSWERS

What are the benefits of tree trimming?
Tree trimming promotes healthy growth, enhances the shape of your trees, and reduces the risk of falling branches. It also improves the overall appearance of your landscape.
Can tree trimming harm my tree?
When performed correctly, tree trimming is beneficial as it removes dead or weak branches and encourages healthy growth. Our certified arborists follow best practices to ensure the health of your trees.
How can I tell if my tree needs trimming?
Indicators that your tree needs trimming include overgrown branches, dead limbs, or branches growing too close to power lines or your home. We offer free assessments to determine your tree’s trimming needs.
