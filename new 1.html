FAQs AND ANSWERS

What's the benefit of tree trimming?
Tree trimming fosters healthy growth, enhances the shape of your trees, and minimizes the risk of falling branches. It also improves the overall look of your landscape.
Can tree trimming damage my tree?
When done correctly, tree trimming is beneficial for your tree as it removes dead or weak branches and promotes healthy growth. Our certified arborists ensure proper trimming practices.
How do I know if my tree needs trimming?
Signs that your tree needs trimming include overgrown branches, dead limbs, or limbs growing too close to power lines or your home. We provide free assessments to determine your tree’s needs.
